# Rental Activity Tracker

A simple, mobile-friendly web app for landlords to track **time, mileage, and expenses** across rental properties.  
Designed to generate **IRS-compliant logs** that support real estate professional status and provide CPA-ready exports.  

---

## Features

- **Quick Add** – log Time, Expense, Mileage, or Tenant Events in seconds  
- **Timer** – start/stop work sessions, auto-calculate hours  
- **Mileage Tracking** – input origin/destination, auto-calculate miles or enter manually  
- **Expense Capture** – upload receipts, OCR vendor/date/amount, tie to property + category  
- **Tenant Management** – log screenings, lease prep, renewals, complaints  
- **Reports** – monthly/yearly rollups of hours, miles, and expenses by property & category  
- **CSV Export** – clean exports for Schedule E or QuickBooks  

---

## Categories

Activities are grouped to align with IRS definitions of active rental participation:  

- **Tenant Management** – listings, inquiries, screening, leases, disputes  
- **Repairs** – DIY work, contractor supervision, materials trips  
- **Operations** – rent collection, utilities, insurance, property taxes, driving  
- **Admin** – bookkeeping, reconciliation, CPA prep  
- **Compliance** – lease/code reviews, legal/accounting communications  

---

## Tech Stack (planned)

- **Frontend:** React / Expo (mobile-first)  
- **Backend:** Supabase (Postgres + Auth + File storage)  
- **OCR:** Tesseract or AWS Textract for receipts  
- **Exports:** CSV and QuickBooks templates  

---

## Why This Exists

Landlords often miss out on deductions or fail to prove **material participation** for IRS audits.  
This tracker creates a clean **audit trail** with timestamps, receipts, mileage logs, and activity categories.  
Think of it as a **Landlord Ledger**: simple, audit-proof, and CPA-friendly.
